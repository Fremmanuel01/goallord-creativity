ABOUT GOALLORD CREATIVITY

We Build Digital Products
That Move Businesses Forward

Founded in 2020 in Onitsha, Anambra State, Goallord Creativity is a 5-person agency that builds websites, web applications, and digital products for businesses across Nigeria and internationally. We work with schools, hospitals, universities, and organizations that need digital products built right.

EMMANUEL KENECHUKWU NWABUFO

Founder & CEO, Goallord Creativity Limited

THE FOUNDER'S STORY

How It Started

I started Goallord Creativity in 2020, right in the middle of COVID. I had a small office in Onitsha, a laptop, and the conviction that businesses around me deserved better websites than what they were getting.

My first clients were schools and hospitals nearby. All Hallows Seminary needed a website. St. Charles Borromeo Hospital needed one too. I built them, and the work spoke for itself. One school told another, one hospital referred the next. That is how we grew. No advertising, no cold calls. Just good work and word of mouth.

By 2022 we had expanded beyond WordPress into full custom development with React, Node.js, and PostgreSQL. We built sites for Shanahan University and Shanahan Education Foundation. Then came Boys Who Write, our first international client, and that changed my perspective on what this agency could become.

Today, we are a team of 5. We have delivered over 50 projects. And in 2023, I launched Goallord Academy because I wanted to pass on what I had learned. Over 50 students have gone through the program so far.

This is still a young company. But we build like we have something to prove, because we do.

WHAT WE STAND FOR

Mission, Vision & Values

// OUR MISSION

Build digital products that actually work for the businesses that use them

We do not make decorative websites. Every project we take on has to solve a real problem: bring in customers, simplify operations, or establish credibility. If it does not move the business forward, we rethink the approach until it does.

// OUR VISION

Be the agency Nigerian and international businesses trust for digital products

When a school in Anambra, a hospital in Onitsha, or a nonprofit in the UK needs a website or web application, we want Goallord to be the name that comes up. Not because of marketing, but because the work we have done for others was too good to ignore.

Craft Over Speed

We do not rush projects to hit arbitrary deadlines. We build right the first time, so you do not pay twice.

Transparent Pricing

Our websites range from ₦800K to ₦1.2M. You know the cost upfront. No hidden fees, no surprise invoices halfway through.

Long-Term Relationships

We do not build and disappear. Most of our clients have been with us for years. We maintain, update, and grow with you.

Nigerian Roots, Global Standards

We are based in Onitsha and proud of it. But the quality of our work competes with agencies anywhere in the world.

OUR PROCESS

How We Work

Every project follows the same four steps. No shortcuts.

01

Discovery

We learn your business, your goals, and your audience. We study your competitors and figure out exactly what the website or application needs to do.

02

Design

Custom layouts designed in Figma. You approve every detail before a single line of code is written. No templates, no guesswork.

03

Build

Clean, well-structured code. Tested on every device and browser. We use React, Next.js, WordPress, or Shopify depending on what your project requires.

04

Launch & Grow

We go live, set up analytics, and provide ongoing support. Most of our clients stay with us long after launch for updates, SEO, and new features.
