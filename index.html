    Frequently
    Asked Questions

    01

    How long does a website project take?

    WordPress websites typically take 7–21 days. Custom business websites take 2–8 weeks depending on complexity. Web applications take 4–10+ weeks. We provide a detailed timeline in your project proposal before any work begins.

    02

    Do you work with clients outside Nigeria?

    Yes. We work with clients across Africa, Europe, and beyond. All communication, design reviews, and handoffs happen online through structured milestones. We're fully remote-ready and have successfully delivered projects for clients in the UK, Germany, and the UAE.

    03

    What platforms do you build on?

    We specialise in WordPress, Shopify, and custom HTML/CSS/JavaScript websites. For web applications we use React and Node.js. We recommend the right platform based on your goals, budget, and long-term needs.

    04

    What is your payment process?

    We require a 50% deposit to begin work, with the remaining 50% due upon final delivery and your approval. We accept bank transfer and international payments. No hidden fees — the price in your proposal is the price you pay.

    05

    Do you offer ongoing support after launch?

    Yes. Our monthly maintenance plans start at €79/month and include updates, security monitoring, backups, and priority support. We also offer ad-hoc support and feature additions for clients not on a monthly plan.
