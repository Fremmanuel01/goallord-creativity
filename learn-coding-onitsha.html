Why Learn to Code in Onitsha

Southeast Nigeria's Growing Tech Scene

The tech industry in Southeast Nigeria is picking up speed. Onitsha sits at the centre of a region known for business and hustle. That same energy is now flowing into tech. More startups, more agencies, and more businesses need developers and designers. Learning to code here puts you right where the opportunities are growing.

Earn in Dollars from Onitsha

Remote work has changed the game. Web developers and designers in Nigeria now work for clients in the US, UK, and Europe without ever leaving home. You can live in Onitsha, keep your costs low, and earn in foreign currency. Our graduates are already doing this.

Lower Cost of Living, Higher Returns

Compared to Lagos, your money goes further in Onitsha. Rent, food, and transport cost less. That means you can invest in learning programming without the financial pressure of a big city. And once you start earning as a developer, the margins are even better.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does coding training cost in Onitsha?

At Goallord Academy, in-person coding training costs ₦150,000 for the full 12-week programme. We also offer an online option at ₦80,000. Installment payments are available for the in-person track, split into 3 monthly payments of ₦50,000. This covers all course materials, mentoring, certificate, and job placement support.

Can I learn programming without any experience?

Yes. Most of our students start with zero coding experience. Our curriculum begins with the fundamentals and builds up week by week. By week 4, you will be writing real code. By week 12, you will have built multiple projects you can show to employers or clients.

How long does it take to learn web design?

Our web design training runs for 12 weeks. Within that time, you will learn HTML, CSS, JavaScript, responsive design, and how to build complete websites. Most students are comfortable taking on freelance projects within 2-3 months after completing the programme.

Is there a coding school near me in Onitsha?

Goallord Academy is located at No. 1 Mission Road, Onitsha, Anambra State. We are one of the few dedicated coding schools in the Onitsha area, offering hands-on web design and programming training with small class sizes of 10-15 students per batch.

What programming languages should I learn first?

We recommend starting with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. These are the foundation of web development and the most in-demand skills for entry-level tech roles in Nigeria. Our web design track covers all three, plus frameworks like React and tools like Node.js for backend development.

Can I get a job after learning to code at Goallord?

85% of our graduates land paid work within 3 months of completing the programme. We provide job placement support, portfolio reviews, interview prep, and access to our alumni network. Many graduates freelance for clients in Nigeria and abroad, while others join agencies and tech companies.
